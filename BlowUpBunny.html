Healthy Bodies - Installation concept series - #3 Blow Up Bunny

This series is an exploration of form and scale in relation to the body. I use ceramic as a medium to render shapes that invite people to reimagine their connection to their surroundings. Each piece is intentionally made to be examined from every angle and create an invitation to touch purely based on their form.

Form

This piece balances solidity with a sense of lightness, with a textured surface that looks soft and inviting to the touch. Its abstract shape hints at the familiar, evoking the approachable essence of an animal while remaining open to interpretation. The form invites exploration, encouraging viewers to engage with it from every angle.
