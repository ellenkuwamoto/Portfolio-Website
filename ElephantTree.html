Healthy Bodies - Installation Concept Series - #2 Elephant Tree

This series is an exploration of form and scale in relation to the body. I use ceramic as a medium to render shapes that invite people to reimagine their connection to their surroundings. Each piece is intentionally made to be examined from every angle and create an invitation to touch purely based on their form.

Form

This piece is designed to be experienced both upright and upside down, offering multiple interpretations of its shape. Drawing inspiration from natural elements such as tree roots and elephant trunks, it blurs the lines between plant and animal forms. The three-legged structure adds a sense of stability and balance, grounding the piece while inviting curiosity and interaction.
