ELLEN KUWAMOTO Work

Student, Artist, Designer

I am currently a sophmore at NYU studying Interactive Media Arts originally from San Francisco. I use multimedia arts to explore the relationship between function, tangability, and communal relations. I believe that the physical practice of making art is foundationally human, and I use my work to explore how we cultivate these experiences to bring ourselves together. From personalized ceramic mugs meant for sharing drinks to digital pieces derived from organic functions, I use my work to form experiences out of my careful detail-driven practice.
