ELLEN KUWAMOTO About 3D Work Creative Coding

Maker of tactile shapes

I work at the intersection of interaction and objecthood, exploring the inner relationship between the body and its environment. Whether through sculpture, design, or digital works, I aim to create forms that help people forge meaningful connections with their surroundings. I strive to instill a sense of mindfulness in how individuals engage with my creations. Whether working with tangible materials or digital interfaces, I want my works to draw people to use and touch them—even when physical touch isn’t possible.
