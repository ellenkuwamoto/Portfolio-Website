Healthy Bodies - Installation concept series - #4 Anthill Globs

This series is an exploration of form and scale in relation to the body. I use ceramic as a medium to render shapes that invite people to reimagine their connection to their surroundings. Each piece is intentionally made to be examined from every angle and create an invitation to touch purely based on their form.

Form

Inspired by the construction of anthills and bird nests, this piece features a globular form that appears as a collection of smaller parts unified into a cohesive whole. Designed to be oriented either right side up or upside down, it invites the viewer to exploreand interpret its organic shape from multiple perspectives.
