Inspiration

This organic lamp concept draws from the visionary work of artist Olafur Eliasson, inspired by his exploration of futurism, environmentalism, and his manipulations of light. I wanted to reimagine his philosophy on a smaller, more accessible scale and imagine ways to bring light installation to a new audience.

Target Audience

This organic lamp is perfect for desk workers, science enthusiasts, and those passionate about the environment. Designed for individuals seeking to brighten their home or workspace sustainably, it offers a unique way to engage with light and design without wasting materials or electricity. Functionality

This lamp concept features a tower structure that can be easily assembled from 2D parts, with an outer cover crafted from bioplastics. Designed to refract light, the bioplastic creates a vibrant, colorful glow when exposed to natural sunlight. The abstract design uses the power of light to bring a dynamic, energy-free ambiance into the home, without the need for electricity.
