Healthy Bodies - Installation concept series - #1 Wind Bone

This series is an exploration of form and scale in relation to the body. I use ceramic as a medium to render shapes that invite people to reimagine their connection to their surroundings. Each piece is intentionally made to be examined from every angle and create an invitation to touch purely based on their form.

Form

This piece draws inspiration from buried artifacts, blending inspirations from instruments, bones, and abstract forms into a singular, intriguing object. Its hollow structure and tactile presence invite viewers to imagine its weight and purpose, evoking a deep desire to pick it up and engage with it physically.
