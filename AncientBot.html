Healthy Bodies - Installation concept series - #5 Ancient Bot

This series is an exploration of form and scale in relation to the body. I use ceramic as a medium to render shapes that invite people to reimagine their connection to their surroundings. Each piece is intentionally made to be examined from every angle and create an invitation to touch purely based on their form.

Form

This piece juxtaposes geometric precision with an exterior softened by moss and the marks of time, evoking a sense of history and decay. It invites viewers to imagine the life it once lived, its purpose, and the stories it holds. The form exudes stillness and meditation, encouraging quiet reflection on the passage of time and the fusion of the mechanical and the natural.
